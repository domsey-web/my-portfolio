Website Design for vintage fashion brand
'Thrifty Store'

For this project I created a design and prototype for Thrifty Store, a vintage brand that didn't have an e-commerce website. I went through the research process which involved collecting data through surveys and questionnaires in order to confirm my target audience, and gather information on participants' online shopping experiences and preferences.

After this, I was able to create wireframes and concept designs before moving onto mock ups and final designs. When designing the website, a conscious effort was made to include web design trends such as aspects minimalism and symmetry, as well as tying in the brand's identity which revolved around the colour yellow and 70s, 80s and 90s imagery.
