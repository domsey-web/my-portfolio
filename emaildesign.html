Email Design

One part of my current job role involves me regularly designing and building HTML emails - subjects ranging from:

  • marketing emails
  • special offers
  • service emails
  • new product releases

The design process often begins with a theme - particularly in the lead up to big holidays and special events throughout the year. I would center the design around a particular theme, colour scheme, or central product. It was important for these emails to be eye catching, attention grabbing. This meant that I often worked with bright colour palettes to make the emails fun, attractive, and interesting to look at - but also keeping the brand guidelines in mind.. This was great for me, as I was able to be creative and inventive with email designs, and was able to insert some humour at times into my work.

As well as designing emails, I would also build the emails using Adobe Dreamweaver. After proof reading and consulting with the merchandising and gifting teams on products being showcased, the email would then be built on CCMP and scheduled for release.
